Coq Au Vin

Description

Coq au vin is a French dish of braised chicken, lardons, mushrooms, and optionally garlic. In a thick rich red Burgundy wine sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 onces bacon or pancetta
  • 1 (3 to 4 pound) chicken, cut in 8ths
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 1/4 cup of Cognac or brandy
  • 1/2 bottle good dry red wine such as Burgundy
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 10 fresh thyme sprigs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 pound frozen small white onions
  • 1/2 pound cremini mushrooms, thickly sliced
